About Me

Hello! I'm David Wright, I'm a software engineer who loves learning and building things. I've been full stack focused in a variety of languages with my favorite projects being a real time inventory streaming application built with Apache Flink and an interactive store layout planning tool built in vanilla Javascript.

In mid 2023 I made the decision to take some time off work to travel the world with my significant other. So far its been a terrific experience and I've had time to do deep dives into topics that I've been curious about but have never had the mental energy to dig into after a normal work day. I'll keep this blog updated with my travels and any recommendations I have for others who might wish to do something similar.

In the future I'd like to work on projects where the target audience is a large population. The problems I've worked on professionally have been large scale, but the target audiences have always been a known number of corporate users. I'd be excited to work on something which is used by millions of people, especially if its a product which can be improved by interacting directly with users.
