Background

Hello, and welcome to my blog! My name is David Wright, and I am a software engineer who enjoys UX and building things.

When I'm not working on software projects, you can often find me outdoors. I'm an avid cyclist and love exploring new routes on my bike whether that be through the city or through the fields while racing cyclocross. One of my biggest goals is to one day climb Denali, and I'm currently training to make that dream a reality.

I also enjoy playing chess and learning languages in my spare time. I don't play as much as I'd like, but I'm addicted to Chess puzzles having completed over 2,000 so far. You can challenge me username "bloodisblue" on chess.com.

Through this blog, I hope to share my experiences, insights, and my adventures in the great outdoors. Thanks for stopping by!
